Mental Health App

An initiative to digitalize treatment plans for young people struggling with addiction, helping them to keep time of their psychologist appointments, track their progress and reach out to a pofessional if they need help. This was done through accessible UX & UI with large respect for the target audience and their personal needs.

Finding my visual voice

As a part of the Hyper Island Digital Creative Program, we got to, together with artist and designer Benedetta Crippa, explore our own personal brand and visual voice. This was done through multiple woprkshops, guest lectures and self- exploration and resulted in a class exhibition where we got to present what makes our artistic and professional visions unique to us.

Branding and Marketing Project

As part of our Branding-course, I got the opportunity to project lead a re-brand for a major company within the construction industry. My team did everything from pick out new brand colors, change the logo, prepare SOMO strategy, pitch decks as well as a website mockup. We gathered it all in a comprehensive brand book and were met with very positive feedback.

Night Club App

A UX project to make nightlife in Stockholm more accessible and meaningful, including a list over clubs and events happening in the area, option to save favorites and a filter system based on personal interests, music taste and intention for the night.
